Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name

Working Name: Revues
(Rename later; name not critical for MVP)

Product Type

SaaS Web Application
B2B (Business Owners) + Public Customer Review Flow

1. Product Vision

Revues helps businesses collect more authentic, high-quality Google reviews by removing friction for customers through a guided, AI-assisted experience:

Scan → Click → Review

The product assists real customers in expressing real experiences faster.
It does not fabricate, auto-post, or manipulate Google reviews.

2. Problem Statement
The Real Problem

Customers don’t leave reviews because it’s inconvenient.

When they do, reviews are short, vague, or abandoned.

Businesses want better review quality without risking Google policy violations.

What Revues Solves

Reduces review friction to under 60 seconds

Helps customers articulate feedback clearly

Increases review completion rates

Improves review richness (useful context for Google Business Profiles)

Explicit Non-Goals

No fake reviews

No automated posting

No incentives

No keyword stuffing

No Google API manipulation in V1

3. Target Users
Business Users (Primary)

Local businesses (restaurants, dental clinics, gyms, salons, contractors)

Owners or managers (non-technical)

End Customers (Secondary)

Real customers scanning a QR code

4. Core Value Proposition

“Make it effortless for real customers to leave thoughtful Google reviews.”

Key benefits:

Faster reviews

Higher completion rate

Better-written, more descriptive feedback

Safe, compliant workflow

5. High-Level User Flows
Business Owner Flow

Sign up / log in

Add business details

Insights & Data Preview (NEW)

Select review focus areas

Generate QR code

Share QR with customers

Customer Flow

Scan QR

Choose experience type

Select what stood out / describe concern

AI-assisted draft is generated

Customer edits, confirms, copies

Redirect to Google review page

6. Compliance & Safety Requirements (MANDATORY)

AI output must always be:

Editable

User-confirmed

No auto-posting to Google

Clear disclaimer on every AI review:

“This is a suggested draft based on your input. Please edit to reflect your experience.”

Customers choosing “Concern” must still be allowed to leave a Google review

No forced language, keywords, or sentiment

11. Customer Review Flow (Public)
Step 1: Experience Selection

Route:

/r/:businessSlug/experience

Options:

Great experience

I have a concern

Step 2A: Great Experience

Route:

/r/:businessSlug/feedback

Select up to 4 chips

Optional free-text (120 chars)

Step 2B: Concern Flow (CRITICAL)

Still allows Google review.

Display two options:

Tell the business privately

Leave a Google review

AI tone must be:

Calm

Factual

Non-defamatory

Step 3: AI Review Generation

Route:

/r/:businessSlug/review

AI Requirements:

2–5 sentences

Natural, human tone

No emojis

No exaggeration

Contextual details only if user-provided

Regeneration Limits (MANDATORY)

Max 3 regenerations

Counter tracked server-side

Optional cooldown between regenerations

After limit:

Regenerate disabled

Manual editing allowed

Step 4: Confirmation

Route:

/r/:businessSlug/confirm

Required checkbox:

“I confirm this reflects my experience.”

Buttons:

Copy Review & Continue

Regenerate (if available)

Step 5: Redirect

Opens Google review link in new tab

Clear instruction to paste and submit
